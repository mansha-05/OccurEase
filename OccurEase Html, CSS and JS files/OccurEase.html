This site offers a range of features and tools to help event organizers streamline their processes and enhance attendee experiences. With OccurEase Event Management, users can create and customize event pages, set up online registration and ticketing, and manage attendee information securely.

From Ordinary to Extraordinary experience events like never Before

Event Creation
Essential details such as event name, date, time, location, and event description can be added on our site. You can also add additional information like speakers, sponsors, agenda, and ticket types.
Registration and Ticketing
OccurEase enables an online registration and ticketing system. Attendees can easily register for the event and purchase tickets through a user-friendly interface. Organizers can customize ticket types, pricing, availability, and any special offers or discounts.
Event Promotion
OccurEase offers various tools and features to promote events. Organizers can leverage built-in email marketing, social media integration, and promotional campaigns to reach a wider audience.
Attendee Management
OccurEase provides organizers with a comprehensive attendee management system. They can access attendee lists, collect relevant information during registration, send event-related updates and reminders, and manage cancellations or refunds if necessary.
Payment Processing
OccurEase integrates with secure payment gateways to process ticket sales and handle financial transactions. Organizers can choose their preferred payment gateway and receive payments directly into their accounts or through the OccurEase platform.
Event Check-In
On the day of the event, OccurEase offers convenient check-in features. Organizers can use mobile apps or dedicated check-in tools to scan tickets or check attendees off the registration list, ensuring a smooth entry process.
Analytics and Reporting
OccurEase provides comprehensive analytics and reporting features to help organizers evaluate the success of their events. They can track ticket sales, attendee demographics, engagement metrics, and other relevant data to gain insights and make informed decisions for future events.
Additional Features
OccurEase may offer additional features such as session scheduling, speaker management, exhibitor management, networking tools, event surveys, and integration with other event-related services like event apps or live streaming platforms.
